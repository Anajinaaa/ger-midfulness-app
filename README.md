# Ger — From Digital Peace to Real-World Preservation

> A mindfulness app that funds Mongolian nomadic land rights.

🌐 **Live Demo:** [ger.netlify.app](https://gerr.netlify.app)

---

## What is Ger?

Ger is a mindfulness application inspired by the Mongolian *ger* — the traditional circular dwelling of nomadic herder families. The app gives burned-out urban users a digital sanctuary to decompress, while every session generates micro-donations to protect real nomadic grazing land through the **International Land Coalition (ILC)**.

The core idea: **your stillness becomes their land.**

---

## The Problem

- 📱 The average adult spends **4.2 hours/day** on screens
- 😰 **70%** of adults report feeling chronically overstimulated
- 🏔 **40%** of Mongolia's traditional nomadic grazing land has been lost in 20 years

Two crises — digital burnout and cultural erasure — have never been connected. Until now.

---

## How It Works

| Step | What Happens |
|------|-------------|
| 1 | User opens Ger and enters a digital nomadic sanctuary |
| 2 | They complete mindful "chores" — stoking a fire, tending the herd |
| 3 | Time spent in the app generates micro-donations |
| 4 | Funds flow to the ILC to secure nomadic grazing rights in Mongolia |
| 5 | User sees real impact — exact square metres of land protected |

## Features

- 🔥 Animated Mongolian steppe environment with interactive fire, herd, and ger

## Why Ger vs. Competitors

Inspired by the **Forest app** (10M+ paid users, 2M trees planted), Ger applies the same elegant logic to cultural preservation — with a subscription model that generates 10× the per-user impact funding.

| | Forest | Ger |
|---|---|---|
| Model | $3.99 one-time | $4.99/month |
| Mechanic | Phone away | In-app world |
| Impact | Trees planted | Land secured |
| Cause | Reforestation | Nomadic land rights |
