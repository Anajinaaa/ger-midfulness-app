The model

Simple for users.
Real for the world.

01 💸

Freemium + Premium

Free access to core sessions. Premium ($4.99/mo) unlocks deeper chores, seasonal content, and impact tracking.

02 📊

Time = Funding

Every paid session minute generates a micro-donation. Users see exactly how many square metres of grazing land their stillness has protected.

03 🤝

ILC Partnership

Funds flow directly to the International Land Coalition's Mongolia programme — a verified, transparent, non-profit land rights body.

Users receive real updates: "Your 14 hours this month secured 11m² for the Khalkha herder community in Övörkhangai." Impact becomes personal.

Differentiation

Purpose-native, not purpose-added

Headspace and Calm raised hundreds of millions and then bolted on social impact as an afterthought. Ger's entire product logic is inseparable from its cause. You can't have one without the other.

Retention

Emotional stakes create habits

Forest users replant because they don't want their tree to die. Ger users return because they're genuinely sustaining something — a real family, a real herd, a real landscape. The guilt mechanics are positive, not punitive.
